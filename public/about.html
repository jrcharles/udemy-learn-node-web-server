About

JR Charles has been an entrepreneur and strategy consultant since 2010. His fields of experience span from designing and implementing technology systems utilizing the latest in computer hardware multi- site networking and remote systems and cloud computing to sophisticated e-commerce and marketing implementations from multiple industries including: Sporting Goods, Apparel, Home & Gardening, Non-Profits, Professional Services, and Art and Crafts.

As a strategy consultant, JR has consulted with over 50 companies. His work has also included numerous startups and VC-backed startups and he played interim VP, C-level roles of several of these ventures. His work focused on developing and executing marketing plans, designing easy-to- use functional web and mobile apps, and e-commerce business model planning.

As an entrepreneur CEO, JR has founded two companies, most recent being ParOneTV, Inc (The first ever, CASN, content aggregation, syndication network for golf where content providers can use this technology to scale their own branded content advertising campaigns). Prior to that JR worked as a business owner in the home-automation space and learned the expertise and skills of designing and building hardware and software.
